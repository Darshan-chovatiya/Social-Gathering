Understanding our refund and cancellation procedures

Our Refund Policy

At Social Gathering (operated by ITFuturz), we understand that circumstances may change. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for ticket cancellations and refunds. Please read this policy carefully before making a booking.

Refund Policy Details

Ticket Purchases: All ticket purchases are generally final and non-refundable unless otherwise stated in this policy or required by applicable law.

Event Organizer's Policy: Refund eligibility is primarily determined by the event organizer's refund policy. Each event may have different refund terms, which will be clearly displayed during the booking process.

Service Fees: Service fees and processing charges are typically non-refundable, even if the ticket price is refunded.

Full Refund: If an event is cancelled by the organizer, you will be entitled to a full refund of the ticket price paid.

Automatic Processing: Refunds for cancelled events are typically processed automatically within 7-14 business days to the original payment method.

Notification: You will be notified via email about the cancellation and refund process. Please ensure your contact information is up to date.

Service Fees: In case of event cancellation, service fees may or may not be refunded depending on the organizer's policy.

Ticket Validity: If an event is postponed or rescheduled, your tickets will typically remain valid for the new date.

Refund Option: If you cannot attend the rescheduled event, you may be eligible for a refund. Refund requests must be submitted within the timeframe specified by the organizer (usually 7-30 days after the new date is announced).

No Action Required: If you can attend the rescheduled event, no action is needed - your tickets will automatically be valid for the new date.

Cancellation Window: Some events may allow cancellations within a specified time frame (e.g., 24-48 hours before the event). Check the event details for specific cancellation policies.

Refund Eligibility: Refunds for customer-initiated cancellations are subject to the event organizer's policy and may incur cancellation fees.

Processing Time: If approved, refunds will be processed within 7-14 business days to the original payment method.

No-Show Policy: If you fail to attend an event without prior cancellation, you are not entitled to a refund.

Processing Period: Approved refunds are typically processed within 7-14 business days from the date of approval.

Payment Method: Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used for the purchase (credit card, debit card, UPI, etc.).

Bank Processing: After we process the refund, it may take additional time (3-5 business days) for your bank or payment provider to credit the amount to your account.

Refund Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed.

The following items are typically non-refundable:

  • Service fees and processing charges
  • Convenience fees
  • Gift cards or promotional vouchers (unless required by law)
  • Tickets for events marked as "No Refund" or "Final Sale"
  • Special packages or add-ons that are explicitly marked as non-refundable

Please review the event details carefully before completing your purchase.

Online Request: Log in to your Social Gathering account and navigate to your booking history. Click on the booking for which you want a refund and follow the refund request process.

Email Request: Send an email to our support team at info@itfuturz.com with your booking ID, event details, and reason for refund request.

Phone Support: Call us at +91 6354390540 during business hours to speak with our support team about your refund request.

Required Information: Please provide your booking ID, full name, email address, and reason for the refund request.

Partial Refund Eligibility: In some cases, partial refunds may be available depending on the event organizer's policy and the circumstances of the cancellation.

Cancellation Fees: Partial refunds may be subject to cancellation fees or administrative charges as specified by the event organizer.

Multiple Tickets: If you purchased multiple tickets and only need to cancel some, partial refunds may be available for the cancelled tickets only.

Chargeback Policy: If you initiate a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider, we will investigate the matter. Unauthorized chargebacks may result in account suspension.

Resolution Process: We encourage you to contact our support team first to resolve any issues before initiating a chargeback. We are committed to resolving disputes fairly and promptly.

Documentation: Please provide all relevant documentation and details when reporting a disputed transaction to help us resolve the issue quickly.

Medical Emergencies: In case of medical emergencies preventing attendance, please contact our support team with appropriate documentation. We will review each case individually.

Natural Disasters: If an event is cancelled due to natural disasters or force majeure events, refund policies will be determined on a case-by-case basis.

Government Restrictions: If events are cancelled due to government restrictions or regulations, standard cancellation refund policies will apply.

Contact Support: For special circumstances not covered in this policy, please contact our support team, and we will work with you to find a fair solution.
