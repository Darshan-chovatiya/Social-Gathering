About Easy Ticket

Simplifying event discovery and ticket booking across India

Who We Are

Easy Ticket is a fast, secure, and user-friendly online event ticketing platform operated by ITFuturz. We help users discover, book, and attend concerts, workshops, parties, sports events, seminars, and cultural programs across India with complete ease.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make event participation simple, transparent, and enjoyable. We aim to eliminate long queues, complex booking steps, and hidden charges by providing a seamless digital ticketing experience for both event organizers and attendees.

What We Provide

Secure Payments

Safe and trusted payment gateways supporting UPI, cards, and net banking.

Digital E-Tickets

Instant QR-code based tickets for fast and contactless event entry.

Organizer Dashboard

Powerful tools to manage events, track sales, and monitor attendees.
